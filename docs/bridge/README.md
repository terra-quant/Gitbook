# Bridge

## What is Xhavic Bridge?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#what-is-base-bridge) <a href="#what-is-base-bridge" id="what-is-base-bridge"></a>

Xhavic Bridge enables the transfer of certain digital assets and other data back and forth between Ethereum and Xhavic.

## What wallet can I use?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#what-wallet-can-i-use) <a href="#what-wallet-can-i-use" id="what-wallet-can-i-use"></a>

You can use popular Ethereum wallet like MetaMask.

## What if I have a question, issue or problem?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#what-if-i-have-a-question-issue-or-problem) <a href="#what-if-i-have-a-question-issue-or-problem" id="what-if-i-have-a-question-issue-or-problem"></a>

The [Xhavic Discord](https://discord.gg/HkXHH9g5) community is available around the clock for general questions, assistance and support!

## Can I move my assets back to Ethereum from Xhavic?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#can-i-move-my-assets-back-to-ethereum-from-base) <a href="#can-i-move-my-assets-back-to-ethereum-from-base" id="can-i-move-my-assets-back-to-ethereum-from-base"></a>

Yep! The process is bidirectional, you can bridge from both Ethereum to Xhavic, and Xhavic to Ethereum.

## How long does the transfer take?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#how-long-does-the-transfer-take) <a href="#how-long-does-the-transfer-take" id="how-long-does-the-transfer-take"></a>

Transferring from Ethereum to Xhavic usually only takes a few minutes, but that timeline is not guaranteed. Transferring from Xhavic to Ethereum takes approximately 7 days. This is a security feature designed to help secure Xhavic . If you prefer not to wait, you can alternatively use a third-party bridge which allows you to transfer assets much faster!

## How do you withdraw from Xhavic?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#how-do-you-withdraw-from-base) <a href="#how-do-you-withdraw-from-base" id="how-do-you-withdraw-from-base"></a>

Connect your wallet and confirm that it is set to Xhavic network. Choose the digital asset you wish to bridge back to Ethereum testnet and confirm you want to withdraw.

After your withdrawal request is proposed onchain (within an hour) you must verify and complete the transaction in order to access your funds. You can track your progress under the transaction tab.

Transferring from Xhavic to Ethereum takes approximately 7 days. This is a security feature designed to help secure Xhavic. If you prefer not to wait, you can alternatively use a third-party bridge which allows you to transfer assets much faster.

## Are there any fees involved in using Xhavic Bridge?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#are-there-any-fees-involved-in-using-base-bridge) <a href="#are-there-any-fees-involved-in-using-base-bridge" id="are-there-any-fees-involved-in-using-base-bridge"></a>

There are network fees involved which are used to pay for the gas costs on Ethereum and Xhavic. There are no additional fees specifically for using Xhavic Bridge. You can find more information on how network fees work on Xhavic here.

## What is the benefit of transferring my digital assets from Ethereum to Xhavic?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#what-is-the-benefit-of-transferring-my-digital-assets-from-ethereum-to-base) <a href="#what-is-the-benefit-of-transferring-my-digital-assets-from-ethereum-to-base" id="what-is-the-benefit-of-transferring-my-digital-assets-from-ethereum-to-base"></a>

By transferring digital assets from Ethereum to Xhavic, you get to experience the scalability and efficiency of Xhavic-- this means cheaper and faster transactions, reduced gas fees, and all of the DApps unique to the network!

## Can I use the same wallet on Xhavic that I use on Ethereum?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#can-i-use-the-same-wallet-on-base-that-i-use-on-ethereum) <a href="#can-i-use-the-same-wallet-on-base-that-i-use-on-ethereum" id="can-i-use-the-same-wallet-on-base-that-i-use-on-ethereum"></a>

Yes, in most cases you can use the same Ethereum-compatible wallet to interact with Xhavic.

## Are there any risks involved in using Xhavic Bridge?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#are-there-any-risks-involved-in-using-base-bridge) <a href="#are-there-any-risks-involved-in-using-base-bridge" id="are-there-any-risks-involved-in-using-base-bridge"></a>

While every effort is made to ensure the security and reliability of the bridge, it's important to understand that all blockchain transactions involve some degree of risk.

## Can I use the Xhavic Bridge if I'm new to blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#can-i-use-the-base-bridge-if-im-new-to-blockchain-technology-and-cryptocurrencies) <a href="#can-i-use-the-base-bridge-if-im-new-to-blockchain-technology-and-cryptocurrencies" id="can-i-use-the-base-bridge-if-im-new-to-blockchain-technology-and-cryptocurrencies"></a>

The bridge is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, even for those new to the technology. However, it's always recommended to have a basic understanding of blockchain transactions and best practices. If you're unsure, it might be beneficial to seek advice or further learning.

## Can I interact with DApps on Xhavic using my transferred digital assets?[​](https://docs.base.org/tools/bridge-faq#can-i-interact-with-dapps-on-base-using-my-transferred-digital-assets) <a href="#can-i-interact-with-dapps-on-base-using-my-transferred-digital-assets" id="can-i-interact-with-dapps-on-base-using-my-transferred-digital-assets"></a>

Yes, once your digital assets are on Xhavic, you can interact with dapps deployed on it!
